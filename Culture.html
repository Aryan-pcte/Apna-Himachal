Culture


Kullu Dussehra

  • The most famous festival of Himachal Pradesh, celebrated in Kullu.
  • Unlike other parts of India, it begins after Dussehra.
  • Deities from different regions gather, showcasing rich religious and cultural traditions.

Minjar Mela

  • Celebrated in Chamba, Minjar marks the harvest season.
  • People offer maize flowers (Minjar) and enjoy folk songs, dances, and fairs.

Shivratri Mela

  • A major religious fair where hundreds of deities assemble in Mandi, making it culturally and spiritually significant.
