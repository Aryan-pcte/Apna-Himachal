Popular Mountains


  • Himachal Pradesh is a serene mountain state of India, nestled in the lap of the Western Himalayas.
  • Known for its lofty peaks, deep valleys, and flowing rivers, Himachal represents the true essence of Himalayan geography.



Details

HIMADRI-Mountain Range


  • It is the northernmost and highest Himalayan range influencing Himachal Pradesh.
  • The range remains snow-covered throughout the year.
  • It contains important glaciers that feed perennial rivers.
  • The climate is extremely cold with sparse human settlement.
  • It supports alpine vegetation and unique Himalayan wildlife.






Details

SHIVALIKS-Mountain Range


  • The Shivalik Range is the southernmost and lowest Himalayan range in Himachal Pradesh.
  • It extends across Una, Kangra, Hamirpur, Solan, and Sirmaur districts.
  • The hills are made of soft sedimentary rocks, causing erosion.
  • The region supports agriculture and dense settlements.
  • It forms a natural boundary between plains and higher Himalayas.
